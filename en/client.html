Find the businesses around you

Trendigo makes it easier to visualize local shops.

They are geolocated and there you will find a presentation of their activity as well as the discount they give you.

Merchants who accept trendigo can become part of a community of merchants or have their own loyalty program. All use the same payment system.

Pay without contact

By scanning the QR code at your merchant, you no longer touch any device, you no longer share payment information.

Everything is secure, your cards are encrypted and your transactions are secure.

Receive a percentage of your bills in points, cash equivalents.

Each merchant decides the percentage of cash back they give you on each payment.

This amount is added to your electronic wallet and can be immediately used for another payment. No need to stack or wait to be rewarded!
